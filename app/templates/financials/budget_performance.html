Understanding Cost Performance Index (CPI)

CPI = Earned Value / Actual Cost. A CPI of 1.0 means on budget. >1.0 means under budget (good). <1.0 means over budget (needs attention). For example, CPI of 0.85 means for every $1 spent, only $0.85 of value was earned.
